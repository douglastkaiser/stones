Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 9, 2025

Overview

Your privacy matters. Stones is designed with privacy in mind. We collect no personal data and have no interest in tracking you.

This Privacy Policy explains how Stones ("the App"), developed by Douglas T Kaiser ("we", "us", or "our"), handles your information.

Data We Collect

We collect no personal data. The App does not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Store any data on our servers
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Display advertisements
  • Sell or share data with third parties

Google Play Games Services

If you choose to sign in with Google Play Games Services to access features like leaderboards and achievements, your interaction with those services is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not receive or store any personal data from Google beyond what is necessary to display your gaming profile within the app (such as your display name).

Online Multiplayer

When you play online multiplayer games:

  • Game moves are temporarily stored in Firebase to enable real-time gameplay between players
  • Game data is deleted after the game ends
  • No personally identifiable information is stored beyond your display name, which you choose when creating or joining a game

This temporary data storage is essential for the online multiplayer feature to function and is not used for any other purpose.

Local Data Storage

The App may store the following data locally on your device:

  • Game settings and preferences
  • Current game state (for resuming games)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Games Services (optional) - For leaderboards and achievements. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase - For online multiplayer functionality only. Game data is temporary and deleted after games end.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since we collect no personal data from any users, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
